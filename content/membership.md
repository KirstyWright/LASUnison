## Join in minutes

Pick whichever is easiest. Your cover starts the day you join, and you can cancel any time.

## Why members join

Most people join for protection at work — but your membership does far more than that.

::benefits-grid
::

There's plenty more besides, from pensions advice to our holiday centre at Croyde Bay. **[See the full range of UNISON member benefits](https://unison.org.uk/benefits)**.

## What it costs

Your subscription is banded by what you earn, and every member gets exactly the same cover, support and discounts.

## Extra perks for LAS members

::callout{title="A £50 book grant — up to £100 in your first year" icon="gift" href="/book-grants" cta="About book grants" tone="secondary"}
