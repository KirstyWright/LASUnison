**Welcome to the stewards resource page for all station reps, Health & Safety Reps and Learner Reps. This page has been created to help you find the resources you need to carry out your duties.**

Visit the **[UNISON Knowledge Section](http://www.unison.org.uk/knowledge/)** for information on employment rights and key issues affecting people in the workplace and at home. It provides up-to-date and legally checked guidance, answers to common questions, links to useful external sites and access to supporting materials from various UNISON departments. You will need to be logged in to 'My UNISON' to view most documents. You can now subscribe directly to this website through the box at the bottom of this page. Be sure to read the [Branch blog](http://blog.lasunison.com) as it will have important news and updates.

**Bargaining Guides**

Most UNISON branch officers, stewards and safety reps will negotiate with employers at some time.

It is how you protect and promote the interests of your members and create fairness and equality at work.

These in-depth guides, along with our **[training courses](http://www.unison.org.uk/get-involved/learning-development/activists/)** and **[explanation of the process](https://www.unison.org.uk/bargaining)**, give you the tools you need for effective negotiating and bargaining with employers.

[**Click here for the full list of guides**](https://www.unison.org.uk/get-involved/in-your-workplace/key-documents-tools-activists/bargaining-guides/?amp&__twitter_impression=true)
