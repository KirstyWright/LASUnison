Join our branch on Saturday 26th March 2011 where thousands of people will join the TUC march in London to tell the ConDem government that savage spending cuts are unnecessary and unfair.

We don’t believe the ConDems when they say we are all in this together, their public service cuts will hit the most vulnerable the hardest.

Put it in you diary and ask colleagues, friends and family to put it in theirs.
LAS UNISON Branch Committee will be meeting outside LAS HQ Waterloo at 10:00, and then leaving at 10:30 to join other UNISON Ambulance Branches from across the country and our other UNISON Colleagues at 11:00 on Victoria Embankment.

LAS UNISON Members that attend with us, will have there fares paid, but you must bring a valid receipt.
