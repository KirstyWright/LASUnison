**Joint Statement between NHS ambulance Trusts in England and UNISON, GMB and Unite**

Employers and trade unions held further joint discussions on 20 December.  Progress so far and future joint requirements for data collection and analysis were discussed.  It was agreed that there would be more substantive discussions at a joint meeting on 3 January 2014. ENDS
