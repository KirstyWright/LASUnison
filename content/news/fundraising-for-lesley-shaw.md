Lesley Shaw is a Paramedic UNISON member based at Croydon for many years, who suffered a devastating stroke at her home following a night shift.

The stroke has left her with life changing disabilities and a just giving fund has been setup to make essential improvements to her house and her quality of life.

At the last LAS UNISON branch meeting, we agreed to donate £500 as part of a big push toward the £40,000 that is needed to make essential home adaptations including a wet room, new porch, wheelchair ramp and lift.

If you are able to donate any amount it will help get to the £40,000 target. Please visit - [https://www.justgiving.com/crowdfunding/lesleyspage](https://www.justgiving.com/crowdfunding/lesleyspage) [Link no longer available]{.link-unavailable} to donate and get further information.
