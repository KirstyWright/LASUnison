### FREE tuition and qualification for Functional Skills Level 2 in English and Mathematics

We are again offering FREE functional skills courses for members. [Click here to read about the success of our last course.](/las-unison-wins-award-for-securing-qualifications-for-staff)

This is available to any member who does not have a GCSE or equivalent in English and Mathematics and wants to enter a higher education course, including the LAS Paramedic Program.

- Times/Sessions between 4-6 hours, morning and afternoon
- Time scales - Up to a maximum of 45 hours teaching, though to complete ASAP depending on individual learners requirements
- Sessions - Classroom based with a teacher for facilitating and assistance. Further learning is available using iPads
- Initial Assessment - Classroom based to find initial level and future learning required
- Up to 6 hours FS, however if a learner only requires one subject, they need only attend one of the sessions
- Expectations - Exams to be taken for a level 2 functional skills in English and/or numeracy as soon as individual learner is ready, up to 45 hours learning
- Location: UNISON HQ, Euston Road, NW1
- Dates — **Starts from 24 April 2020** for 17 weeks

To get more information and apply for this FREE tuition opportunity just contact our branch Education Officer.
