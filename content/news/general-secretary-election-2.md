**We are pleased to announce that London Ambulance UNISON Branch has nominated Dave Prentis for re-election as General Secretary.**

**The Branch backed Dave because of his impressive record of standing up for our members in Ambulance Services.**

**He is the only candidate with backing from across the whole of our union and therefore the Branch believes that he is the candidate who can bring the whole union together.**

**Dave has always been very supportive of our work and been a strong spokesperson on our behalf when we’ve needed his backing. He has a strong track record, and the Branch felt that now – more than ever – it is vital that our union has leadership with experience that members can trust to make sure their voices are heard.**

**That’s why we’ve chosen to give Dave our support to continue standing up for all of us.**

**Ballot papers will arrive by post in the week commencing November 9th, and the result of the election will be announced on December 17<sup>th</sup> 2015.**

**More information will be sent out to you in due course. In the meantime look out for the ballot papers arriving at your home address.**
