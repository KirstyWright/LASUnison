Please note that the Industrial Action planned for this Thursday 29th January 2015 and 25th February 2015 is SUSPENDED.

This is to allow unions to consult members on a proposal from the Department of Health.

More information regarding the details will be sent out as soon as possible.

Please pass this on to your members on station and workplaces.

**Thank you for all your work regarding LAS UNISON turnout and organisation throughout the past few months.**

Please put Friday 13th March 2015 in the diary as if the offer is rejected nationally we will be taking further action on that day.
