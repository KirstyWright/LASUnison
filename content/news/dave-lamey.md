It is with great sadness that we have to inform you of the death of Dave Lamey, a much loved former colleague based at Croydon and a proud Senior UNISON Rep for the SW Sector for many years.
Dave died in hospital this evening after a short illness.
Our thoughts are with his family at this sad time, especially his daughter Emma, an EAC based at St Helier, and his many friends and colleagues who served with him in the LAS and UNISON.
