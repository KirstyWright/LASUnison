Dear London Ambulance UNISON Branch Members

Ballot papers for this very important election should be sent to your homes address from Monday 3<sup>rd</sup> April. All branch members can vote for Community, Black, Young, Disabled and Greater London seats. Please take part in the internal democracy of your union.

**Your branch executive nominated the following candidates:-**

#### **Community**

Female Seat

Denise CHARLES Female Seat

#### **Black Members**

Kebba MANNEH Male Seat

Elizabeth CAMERON Female Seat

Manjula KUMARI Female Seat

#### **Young Members**

Andrew ANDERSON General Seat

#### **Disabled Members**

Angela HAMILTON General Seat

Kim SILVER Female Seat

#### **Greater London**

Mike DAVEY Male Seat

Rose MINTI-TUTTON Female Seat

Mitsy HARMON-RUSSELL Female Seat

***Your UNISON branch is supporting the above candidates in the UNISON National Executive Council Elections. The Branch encourages you to use your vote to support these candidates.***

***We as a branch support them because they committed to:***

***\* Putting UNISON Members first***

***\* Fighting Cuts and Austerity***

***\* Fighting for fair pay***

***\* Building a union that looks after you***

***\* Growing our union***

***\* Uniting our union and rejecting the politics of division***

***These principles are what you our membership tell us that you value most and therefore they will represent your values on the National Executive Council.***

Regards

Eddie Brand

LAS UNISON Branch Secretary
