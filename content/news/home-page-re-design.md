Regulars to the site will notice another new look for our home page. This has been brought about due to a number of factors. One of them is the inability for our site to display correctly on LAS service computers. There are certain elements of the page that cannot be seen and results in large blank spaces across the page. We know from the stats that at least 30% of you view our site from work and it is important that it renders correctly. For those of you that viewed our site from home and liked our picture carousel, we will bring it back. Just as soon as the service updates from IE6 to 7,8 or even 9! and stops blocking flash.

We have also today launched a brand new section to the website called 'Eric's Blog'. It does what it says on the tin. Eric will update the blog regularly with what he's doing within UNISON and within the service.  The latest post will be fed directly through to the home page and also right here on the news pages.
