The 2011 NEC election ballot opens on Monday 11th April and closes on 13th May.

Ballot papers will be sent to members home address.

It is important the LAS UNISON members use their vote to keep ambulance health issues at the centre of the union’s agenda.
The LAS UNISON Branch Committee supports and have nominated the following people:

#### **National Seats**

#### Eric Roberts – Healthcare Male Seat Ann Moses – Healthcare Female Seat Christine Sullivan – Healthcare Female seat James Anthony – Healthcare General Seat

#### **London Seats**

#### Conroy Lawrence – Greater London Male Seat Kim Silver – Greater London Female Seat Doris Sutton – Greater London Female Seat Irene Stacey – Greater London Low Paid Seat

**Please use your vote**.

**If you have not received your ballot paper by 19th April 2011**
