Today, the LAS UNISON Branch Committee have agreed to sponsor the LAS Wellbeing Hub.

It has been agreed that our Branch will donate £10,000 towards wellbeing hampers for staff that are self-isolating and unwell.

Isolation packs - do you need some help?

Anyone at home who needs an isolation pack, please email your name, address and mobile to Nicola Bullen ASAP.

We now have extra capacity and an efficient delivery team (thankyou Tom Lynch Kier MacLean and team CRU) and now we also have the support of our UNISON colleagues to sponsor fresh produce and groceries.

To date they have sent out 500 packs and want to continue supporting colleagues, so please get in touch if they can help.
