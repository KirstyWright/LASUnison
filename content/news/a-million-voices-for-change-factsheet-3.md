***What’s happening to the NHS?***

During the recession, the NHS will play a vital role in ensuring that when
people lose their jobs or homes they do not lose access to healthcare.
The NHS is a key part of the fairer society envisaged by UNISON’s
Million Voices campaign, but our health service faces an increasing
threat from privatisation.

Did you know that a far greater role is being created for the private
sector, allowing more companies to take over our services?
