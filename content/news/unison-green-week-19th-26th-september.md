Seed giveaway.

As part of our celebration of UNISON Green Week from 19th to 26th September, we are pleased to offer a limited giveaway of 1000 packets of seeds to promote biodiversity and environmental awareness across our workplaces and communities.

Available seed varieties include: Sunflower, Forget-Me-Not, Poppy, Wildflower, Herb Mixture, Butterfly & Bee Mixture, Cornflower, Lavender, Christmas Tree, Strawberry, and Summer Flower.

These seed packets are available to UNISON members for use either in your workplace or at home.

To request a packet, please contact: Sarah Hardy *UNISON Environment Officer [sarah.hardy27@nhs.net](mailto:sarah.hardy27@nhs.net)*

Let’s grow a greener future, together.
