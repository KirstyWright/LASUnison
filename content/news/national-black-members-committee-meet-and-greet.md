The National Black Members’ Committee has arranged a Meet and Greet session for Black members' who want to learn more about the work of the National Black Members’ Committee (NBMC).

The event will take place on **Friday 21<sup>st</sup>** **May 2021**and can be found on the UNISON webpage [**here**](https://www.unison.org.uk/events/national-black-members-committee-nbmc-meet-and-greet/) [Link no longer available]{.link-unavailable}.

If you have a question or would just like to meet your reps, come along to the relevant Meet and Greet session.

**Session A (11am – 12pm)** is open to Black members from the following regions and Self-Organised Group (SOG) – Eastern, East Midlands, Greater London, Northern, North West, Scotland, and Women’s SOG.

Register for session A here – **https://zoom.us/meeting/register/tJcsde2qrj8jGdIOhs3u0CRFMlD8XJNQ4W99**

**Session B (2pm - 3pm)** is open to Black members from the following regions and Self-Organised Groups (SOGs) – South East, South West, Cymru/Wales, West Midlands, Yorkshire & Humberside, Northern Ireland, LGBT+ and Disabled SOGs

Register for session B here - **https://zoom.us/meeting/register/tJMtde2hqj0rHd1IzUYHvGzRAL9lbfMg8bM6**

To submit questions in advance, please email **[bmca@unison.co.uk](mailto:bmca@unison.co.uk)** stating which session you will be attending in the subject line.
