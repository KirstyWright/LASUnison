Eric Roberts today wrote to Caron Hitchen formally requesting a joint review of Agenda for Change Bandings within the LAS.

Eric said 'As you know Agenda for Change was introduced in 2004 with the majority of evaluation and matching decisions taken in 2005. This review would also give us the opportunity to revisit Job Descriptions and bring them up to date with any workforce planning structures that are agreed'.

As soon as we have more information on the review, it will be posted on this site.
