Christina has worked for UNISON for over 25 years.

She is one of the UK’s most senior women trade unionists - a no non-sense negotiator and a champion for women’s rights, pay equality, justice for low paid workers and a strong supporter of political devolution.

She offers a clear, ambitious, responsible vision for our union’s future to support every UNISON member.

Her election would be a real departure from the past as the first woman General Secretary.
