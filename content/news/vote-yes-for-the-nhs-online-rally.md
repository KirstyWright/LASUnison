## **26 October 2022**

## **6:00pm–6:30pm**

A short online rally is taking place for UNISON members and activists ahead of the NHS pay industrial action ballot, which opens on 27 October.

It’s time to take a stand for your pay, your colleagues and the NHS – every vote is needed. That is why it is so important that all UNISON members working in the NHS **vote yes** for industrial action.

UNISON general secretary Christina McAnea is hosting this rally is to inspire UNISON members and activists to get involved and make your vote count! **#VoteYesfortheNHS**
