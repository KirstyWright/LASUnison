UNISON has launched a new online service to allow you to check your membership details are correct and to update them if necessary.

When times are tough, it's more important than ever that UNISON can contact you - and
your branch members - if we need to.

In April, UNISON's national executive council declared that the scale and nature of the onslaught on public service pensions in particular mean that the union is probably "on the road to industrial action".

We are in talks with the government, but if we have to take action, we need to get our systems right and our membership information as accurate as possible.

Help us to help you by checking your membership details via our new My Details web service. It's secure, quick and easy and you can do it online right now.

Just click, check and update!

Download a bulletin to display in your workplace advertising this great new service.
