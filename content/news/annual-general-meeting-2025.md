This year's AGM will be held at UNISON Centre, 130 Euston Road, London, NW1 2AY.

It starts at 6pm on Wednesday 19th and there will be a buffet provided afterwards.

The AGM is open to all LAS UNISON members, not just reps and officers. As usual, we will be electing Branch Officers and this will be your chance to meet with them and discuss all matters related to our union.

You should, by now, have received an email with details about the event (including the elections). If you have not got this, please email Tim.stephens1@nhs.net

You can also update your details so as not to miss out on future LAS UNISON events, campaigns and news by clicking [here.](/update-your-details)
