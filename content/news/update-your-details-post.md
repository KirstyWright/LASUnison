**UNISON** and the **London Ambulance Branch** want to make sure we have all our members details up to date.

At this current time there is a lot going on and we want to ensure we have your correct contact details?

Have you recently moved house? Changed your email or phone number? Moved to a new station, control room or recently been posted from training?

We may not have your current up to date details so it is vital you update your details.

You can do this online at [**MY UNISON**](https://my.unison.org.uk/) - Just login or register and you can update your details straight away.

Alternatively, you can complete our [**online form**](/update-your-details) on our website that will go direct to our membership officer.

Please update your details today! It will only take you a couple of minutes.
