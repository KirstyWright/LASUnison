**The co-chairs of NHS Staff Council have confirmed that NHS Staff Council has received a mandate to proceed with Agenda for Change (AfC) pay structure reform negotiations in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland.**

In [their statement](https://msg.unison.org.uk/c/1osDd74HgWg1gwm39WFqxGjq5ip), the co-chairs confirmed talks will begin next week.

The governments have made commitments that any agreed reform would be effective from 1 April 2026. So, if agreement is reached, backdating will apply. In addition, negotiations will explore the scope for any agreement to cover 2027-28 and 2028-29.

The Health Service Group Executive is overseeing this work, working with devolved health committees in UNISON Cymru and UNISON Northern Ireland.
