The latest pay circular that takes effect from 1 April 2011 has been published and can be viewed here.

The changes to pay points on the pay spine and in the pay bands are:

- A flat rate increase of £250 to pay spine points 1 – 15
- As part of the 2008-2011 negotiated pay agreement, the top pay point of Pay Band 5 (pay spine point 23) increases by 0.33% from 1 April 2011 with consequential adjustments to pay spine points 18, 19, 21 and 22.
- All remaining pay points on the pay spine and the corresponding pay points in the connected pay bands are unchanged from their 1 April 2010 values.
