**If you’re a Newly Qualified Paramedic or have completed the NQP programme within the last 5 years, we want you to complete our ‘NQP survey 2024’ by clicking this [link.](https://survey.alchemer.eu/s3/90703949/Ambulance-Newly-Qualified-Paramedics-NQP-Survey-2024)**

The survey should only take 5 minutes to complete and will be used to find out more about your experiences as an NQP and the issues you face at work. We will use the findings in our discussions with your employers to ensure the programme is being used as intended and identify areas for improvement.
The survey will close on **31 August.** The more responses we get, the stronger our voice is when raising concerns on your behalf.

Please note, this survey is also open to non-members too so feel free to forward on the link to non-member colleagues and encourage them to **[join UNISON](https://gbr01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fmsg.unison.org.uk%2Fc%2F139s397kxNvYClfw6gQlplPUbbGv&data=05%7C02%7Cmatthew.searles%40nhs.net%7C8749a0cc500e42cc696108dcad7dc5d3%7C37c354b285b047f5b22207b48d774ee3%7C0%7C0%7C638576002910583270%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJWIjoiMC4wLjAwMDAiLCJQIjoiV2luMzIiLCJBTiI6Ik1haWwiLCJXVCI6Mn0%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=Bm8HxO1UUP6cU4iu2eStiu4qZ30vTSs%2BmxanFi3onf0%3D&reserved=0)!**
