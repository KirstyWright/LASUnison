Due to the planned ASLEF and RMT industrial action affecting the London Underground on the 15th, we have extended the AGM meeting to be held both in person if members are able to attend and also available online using Microsoft Teams for members not able to travel to UNISON Centre.

All (and only) London Ambulance UNISON Branch members are invited.

If you are able to attend in person, please let us know via the Facebook page

[LAS UNISON Facebook AGM Event page](https://www.facebook.com/events/729707838590816/?ref=newsfeed)

Should you be unable to join in person, please email webmaster@lasunison.com with your name and membership number and you will be sent a link.

We look forward to seeing you on the 15th and a buffet will be provided for those members attending in person.

Original post:

**All members of the LAS UNISON Branch are invited to attend the** **Annual General Meeting.**

**It is important that as many members as possible attend in order to make it a** **representative meeting.**

## **Wednesday 15th March 2023 at** **18:00**

### UNISON CENTRE

### 130 EUSTON ROAD, LONDON, NW1 2AY

The UNISON Centre is on the Euston road in between Kings Cross & Euston stations.

The AGM is the most important meeting of the year for the branch as rule changes and important decisions can be made.

It also gives you the opportunity to discuss issues with branch officers.

Fares can only be claimed with a valid receipt.
Buffet available after meeting.

### Please note:
