## 20 October 2020

## 6:00pm–7:00pm

[**Online**](https://attendee.gotowebinar.com/register/8822892928622028299)

HCPC-registered UNISON members and branch activists are invited to attend UNISON’s webinar: “***HCPC – a Conversation with your Regulator***” on 20th October from 6pm-7pm.

What do you want your regulator to do for you?

The Health and Care Professions Council wants to hear from you.

This is an opportunity for HCPC-registered UNISON members and branch activists to ask questions of the HCPC about how they plan to improve engagement with registrants; improve the processes involved in regulation and what their future strategy looks like.

On the panel will be:

John Barwick, HCPC Chief Executive

Kellie Green, HCPC Professionalism and Upstream Regulation Lead

Chair: Lucy Knightley, practising Operating Department Practitioner and senior UNISON activist.

This is a member only event
