Dear Member,

We are in a time of National Emergency, a time that has not been seen in our lifetime.

Once again our public service workers are on the frontline fighting to keep our communities and our country safe and well. Fighting, against the odds, to keep services running.

LAS UNISON is working hard to continue to provide the support our members need in unprecedented times whether that’s support and guidance on workplace issues around coronavirus, supporting individual members in workplaces across our branch or helping out with welfare concerns.

Please remember whatever your role as a Health Service provider you will all face your challenges and may require Trade Union assistance from us.

Please don't hesitate to contact your Workplace Representatives your Senior Representatives or Branch Committee members, this can be done by searching for our numbers on this website or UNISON Direct on **0800** **085 7857**

Please continue to look for updates on our website.

Thank you again from all at LAS UNISON
