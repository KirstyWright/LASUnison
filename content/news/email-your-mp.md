We are campaigning for an **immediate pay rise of £2,000 for all NHS staff**.

We need your MP to ask the Prime Minister a simple question - why wait?

**[Act now. Email your MP.](http://msg.unison.org.uk/c/18NJGAtqepVaBXlvJr9oxkKL)**

When we **launched our campaign** a few weeks ago many of you asked how you could get involved.

Many of you have already **[taken part in our campaign days](http://msg.unison.org.uk/c/18NJMIGLofH4VoCIkH7SRhBa)** which is great, now we are asking you to **[get in touch with your local MP](http://msg.unison.org.uk/c/18NJOLqxMchImxIrRMs2XVSD)** and ask for their support.

**[Why not give it a go?](http://msg.unison.org.uk/c/18NJQOaka8SlNGOboRMd4Aa6)**

Wherever you are - your support is vital in making sure the case for better NHS pay is heard in the corridors of power.

It is only by securing extra funding from the UK government that we can improve NHS pay in England, and ensure additional funding flows to Northern Ireland, Scotland and Wales.

**Good luck with contacting your MP.**
