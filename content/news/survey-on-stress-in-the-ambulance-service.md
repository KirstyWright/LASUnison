We are planning to do some work around the levels of  stress in the ambulance sector and are seeking more information on the scale and extent of the problem.
To help us with this we are carrying out a survey which you can find here:

[https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/UNISONambulance2014](https://www.surveymonkey.com/s/UNISONambulance2014)

Please help us by completing and circulating this.

Tweet it and share with your friends on facebook.

The results of the survey will be published at health conference and it is likely to generate significant media interest for the ambulance sector, as well as highlighting this extremely important issue.
