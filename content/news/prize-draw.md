**UNISON** and the **London Ambulance Branch** want to make sure we have all our members details up to date. We know staff move around the service, particularly road staff in the early years and we want to ensure we hold the correct details. We are also regulated by the GDPR and the trade union act and as a result we need **all** our members to complete the update form so we can check the information we hold is current and up to date. We have printed thousands of forms and sent them to every workplace and are once again holding a prize draw as an added incentive to complete the form as soon as you receive it.

**We have 4 prizes of £250 gift cards to be spent at Croyde Bay Resort in Devon**

**Plus, 6 Surprise Goody bags.**
