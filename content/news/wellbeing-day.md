LAS UNISON are pleased to see that the London Ambulance Service are to give ALL staff an extra days annual leave (to be called a wellbeing day), as a thank you for the work we have all done over the past year during the COVID-19 pandemic.

LAS UNISON formally raised this with the service at the Staff Council on 27<sup>th</sup> January 2021.

Please read the FAQ's circulated by the service as there are some slightly different rules surrounding this wellbeing day versus a normal annual leave day and it needs to be taken by March 2022.
