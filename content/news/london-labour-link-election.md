Ballot papers will be going out to members who pay into the Affiliated Political Fund (APF) from Monday 5<sup>th</sup> August 2013 to elect a London Representative to sit on the National Labour Link Committee.

LAS UNISON has nominated **Jason Anderson.**

Jason is a Paramedic within the London Ambulance Service and is a member of LAS UNISON.

Please use your vote.
