Yesterday (20th March) we held our Annual General Meeting at UNISON centre in Euston.

We had a terrific turnout and we would like to thank all the members who turned up last night. Disappointingly we were just a few short of being quorate.

This year, Branch officer reports were put together in a colourful booklet. **[You can download a PDF copy here](/docs/mdocs/AGM%20Poster%202020.pdf)**.

Branch Officers presented their reports and an overview of local and national issues was given.

Afterwards was a chance for members to speak to branch officers directly over a few drinks and food.

All members are encouraged to join a local LAS UNISON facebook group to discuss current issues and to check the website regularly for updates.
