The London Ambulance UNISON Branch Committee would like to extend our warmest thanks to all our hard working members for everything they are doing to keep London safe and well.  We, in our branch, know more than most that this is the most difficult time we have experienced in our lives and we recognise how challenging every aspect of all our members lives are right now both at work and at home.  But we will get through this together and our Branch will do all we can to help our members.  Our reps and activists are still available for anything you may need.

Please take a look at our website [https://lasunison.com/](/) to see some of the work we've continued to do collectively.  If you need advice or information please have a look at the Officers contacts page  [https://lasunison.com/branch-officers](/branch-officers) or the Branch Reps page for someone in your area  [https://lasunison.com/branch-officers/branch-representatives](/branch-officers/branch-representatives).  Alternatively UNISON Direct is still available to make contact through however this service is currently online only at  [https://www.unison.org.uk/get-help/online-enquiries](https://www.unison.org.uk/get-help/online-enquiries).

If any of our members have any welfare concerns during this time please don't hesitate to contact us.  Our Branch Welfare page [https://lasunison.com/welfare](/welfare) has all the info on how to contact us and what we can help with.  We also have a specific Covid-19 page with lots of info that may help  [https://www.unison.org.uk/coronavirus-rights-work/](https://www.unison.org.uk/coronavirus-rights-work/)

We wish all our members well and urge everyone to stay safe and look out for one another.

Solidarity.

London Ambulance UNISON Branch Committee
