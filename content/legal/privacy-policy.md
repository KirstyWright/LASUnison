## Who we are

LAS UNISON is the UNISON branch for London Ambulance Service NHS Trust staff. Our website address is [https://lasunison.com](https://lasunison.com).

**Data controller:** LAS UNISON Branch, London Ambulance Service NHS Trust, 220 Waterloo Road, London SE1 8SD.

## What personal data we collect and why

### Contact and enquiry forms

If you use a contact form on this website we collect your name, email address and any information you include in your message. We use this to respond to your enquiry. We do not store enquiry submissions beyond what is necessary to reply to you.

### Membership applications

If you join UNISON through this site you are directed to the national UNISON join form at [my.unison.org.uk](https://my.unison.org.uk). LAS UNISON does not handle or store the personal data you submit there — please refer to [UNISON's privacy policy](https://www.unison.org.uk/privacy-policy/) for how that data is used.

### Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on this site. See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookie-policy) for full details.

### Embedded content

Pages on this site may include embedded content (such as YouTube videos). Embedded content from other websites behaves as if you had visited those websites directly, and those sites may collect data about you, set cookies and track your interaction. See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookie-policy) for more information.

### Analytics

We may use anonymised analytics (such as Google Analytics or a privacy-respecting alternative) to understand how visitors use the site. Where used, this data is aggregated and does not identify individual visitors.

## Who we share your data with

We do not sell, trade or rent personal data to third parties.

We use the following third-party services which may process data as part of delivering this site:

- **Hosting and CDN** — the site is delivered via a modern cloud hosting provider; see their privacy documentation for how server infrastructure processes request data.
- **YouTube** — if you play an embedded video, YouTube (Google) may set cookies and collect data subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Analytics provider** — where analytics are in use, the provider processes anonymised usage data on our behalf.

---

## How long we retain your data

Contact form enquiries are retained only as long as needed to respond to your message, then deleted.

Analytics data is retained in anonymised, aggregated form in accordance with our analytics provider's data retention settings.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your personal data (the "right to be forgotten").
- **Restriction** — ask us to limit how we use your data.
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact the branch at the address below.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)** at [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk) or by calling 0303 123 1113.
