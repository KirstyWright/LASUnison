The branch's International work is about solidarity — standing with trade unionists facing inequality and injustice beyond our own borders, and bringing their causes to the attention of members here in the LAS.

I’m a Health Advisor for 111 in Croydon, South London.

I am the lead rep on site in my workplace and have extensive trade union experience from my former work in the aviation industry.

I liaise with the UNISON INTERNATIONAL COMMITTEE to bring issues of equality and injustice affecting our trade union brothers and sisters abroad to the attention of our branch, so that we may show them solidarity through campaign awareness, fund-raising and promotion here in the LAS.

[Get in touch](/branch) to raise any issues affecting trade unionism around the world and I will raise them with both our branch and the international committee, in order to shine a spotlight on what is happening the world over.
