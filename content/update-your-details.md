If you have changed your workplace, job title, moved house or got married and changed your name, then you need to update your details with us. We are not currently informed by the service if you change any details and unless you tell us we have no way of knowing.

**You can now create an account and view the details we hold about you and change them directly online. Just [click here](https://my.unison.org.uk/) to use this new feature.**

Alternatively use our traditional update online form below.

Please complete all the fields using your new information. We will then update our records. Thanks.

**PLEASE NOTE: THIS FORM IS ONLY FOR MEMBERS OF THE LONDON AMBULANCE SERVICE UNISON BRANCH**

Note: We require all the information in the fields above even if not changed as this helps us identify you. If you have any questions then please refer to our privacy policy.
