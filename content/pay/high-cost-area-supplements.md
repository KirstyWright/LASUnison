#### Table 17: From 1 April 2018 to 31 March 2021

High cost area supplements are a percentage of basic salary, subject to a minimum and maximum payment.

| Area | From | Supplement | Minimum | Maximum |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Inner London | 1 April 2018 | 20% | £4,326 | £6,663 |
| Inner London | 1 April 2019 | 20% | £4,400 | £6,777 |
| Inner London | 1 April 2020 | 20% | £4,473 | £6,890 |
| Outer London | 1 April 2018 | 15% | £3,659 | £4,664 |
| Outer London | 1 April 2019 | 15% | £3,722 | £4,743 |
| Outer London | 1 April 2020 | 15% | £3,784 | £4,822 |
| Fringe | 1 April 2018 | 5% | £1,000 | £1,733 |
| Fringe | 1 April 2019 | 5% | £1,017 | £1,762 |
| Fringe | 1 April 2020 | 5% | £1,034 | £1,791 |
