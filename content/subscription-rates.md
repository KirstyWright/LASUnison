UNISON membership costs less than you might think – costs start at just £1.30 a month. How much you pay for your UNISON membership depends on how much you earn. But all our members get the same benefits, support and exclusive offers.

#### Membership costs in England, Scotland and Wales/Cymru

| **Annual salary** | **Monthly cost** |
| --- | --- |
| Up to £2,000 | £1.30 |
| £2,001-£5,000 | £3.50 |
| £5,001-£8,000 | £5.30 |
| £8,001-£11,000 | £6.60 |
| £11,001-£14,000 | £7.85 |
| £14,001-£17,000 | £9.70 |
| £17,001-£20,000 | £11.50 |
| £20,001-£25,000 | £14.00 |
| £25,001-£30,000 | £17.25 |
| £30,001-£35,000 | £20.30 |
| over £35,000 | £22.50 |

Don't forget you can claim tax relief on 70% of your subscription rate lowering the real term cost.
