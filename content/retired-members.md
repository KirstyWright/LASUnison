After many years working within the NHS as an employee of the London Ambulance Service in whatever capacity and having been a UNISON member during that time, you have now retired.

I know that one of the reasons that you were working for the NHS was because you cared about the people of London that you so ably served. It follows that you , having now retired , are not going to suddenly stop being the person you were.

UNISON respects this and offers the same benefits that you enjoyed when employed by the London Ambulance Service.

These benefits, listed below, are for life:

- UNISON WELFARE
- LEGAL SERVICES
- CROYDE BAY HOLIDAY VILLAGE
- UNISON TRAVEL
- INSURANCE
- CONFIDENTIAL HELP AND ADVICE
- CONVALESCENT FACILITIES

And many other services.

There are a lot of issues that as Retirees we will encounter in our daily lives, for example, continued free access to the NHS and our Pensions continuing to be linked to inflation to name two,

The more members we have the stronger UNISON RETIRED MEMBERS will be, and the more influence we will have.

As your LAS UNISON Retired Members Branch Officer I will be doing all I can to highlight these and any other issues that affect us.

Please contact me if you have any issues or questions that you feel I can address on your behalf, by email ( or mobile if you consider it urgent.)

For further information on the retired section and an application form, please contact me:

E-Mail: [retired@lasunison.com](mailto:retired@lasunison.com) Telephone: 07921 061 274

Thank You.
